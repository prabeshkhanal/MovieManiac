About Us

We Put People First.

MoiveManiac was born out of our love for movies. Discussing it among friends and finding out which movies are in trending always turned into magical conversation. We wanted to share this same feeling with others, so we set out to build MovieManiac.

Since the main inspiration for this website has always been conversation, we encourage our users to engage in conversation with us. We take constructive criticism very seriously so please do not hesitate to provide any criticism or feedback.
